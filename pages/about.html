Our Story

Welcome to 7 Arts Theatre, Harare's premier multi-purpose venue located in the heart of Avondale Shopping Centre. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing world-class facilities for a wide range of events, from corporate functions and award ceremonies to musical theatre productions and church meetings.

Our state-of-the-art venue combines modern technology with African authenticity, creating a unique atmosphere that celebrates both tradition and innovation.
